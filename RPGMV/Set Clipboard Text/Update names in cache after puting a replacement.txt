-Use search replace regex

-We want to start with name+suffix

-For this example line

"や、やっぱり！？アリサさんの声だ……。": "Oh, I knew it! It's Arisa's voice...",

-We will search for アリサさん (Arisa-san) and replace the JP text, and the ENG name, that doesn't have the san

-In regex replace use this:
  +Search: JPnameWithSuffix(.*)EngName(EngSuffix)?
  -アリサさん(.*)(Arisa|Alisa)(-san)?
  +Replace with: ProperName$1ProperName
  -Alisa-san$1Alisa-san

-Result is: "や、やっぱり！？Alisa-sanの声だ……。": "Oh, I knew it! It's Alisa-san's voice...",

So do a ReplaceAll of -san-san to -san

-Repeat for all other important suffixes (chan, sama, etc)
